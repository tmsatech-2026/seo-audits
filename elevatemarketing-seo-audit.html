On-Page SEO Issues

The elements on each page that tell Google what it's about and convince searchers to click through from results.

Critical Missing Meta Descriptions Across All Pages
Evidence

No meta description tag was found on the homepage, SEO page, Google Ads page, Lead Generation page, Social Media page, About page, or Contact page. The homepage has one set in a meta tag but most service pages do not.

Why It Matters

Meta descriptions are your ad copy in search results. Without them, Google auto-generates snippets that are often uncompelling. A well-crafted description can increase CTR by 5-10% -- critical for a marketing agency trying to demonstrate expertise.

Recommendation

Write unique, compelling meta descriptions (150-160 characters) for every active page using Yoast SEO. Each should include the primary keyword, a value proposition, and a call to action.

Critical Title Tags Too Long and Brand-Heavy
Evidence

All titles follow the pattern "[Service] | Elevate Internet Marketing - Results Driven Marketing". This is 60-80+ characters, causing truncation in SERPs. The brand suffix alone is 50 characters, leaving almost no room for keywords.

Recommendation

Shorten titles to under 60 characters. Put the primary keyword first, use a concise brand suffix. Example: "Lead Generation Services Pretoria | Elevate Marketing" instead of "Lead Generation | Elevate Internet Marketing - Results Driven Marketing".

Critical Blog Completely Abandoned (Last Post: 2018)
Evidence

48 blog posts exist, but the most recent is from May 2018. Most posts date to 2010-2012. Topics reference outdated SEO practices and the digital landscape has changed fundamentally since then (mobile-first indexing, Core Web Vitals, AI, E-E-A-T).

Why It Matters

An abandoned blog with 8-year-old content actively hurts credibility. Prospects will question whether you're still in business. Google also favours sites that demonstrate ongoing freshness and expertise.

Recommendation
  • Either remove/noindex the outdated blog posts, or update the best-performing ones with current information
  • Commit to publishing 2-4 new, high-quality posts per month
  • Focus on topics that demonstrate current expertise: AI in marketing, GA4, first-party data, etc.
High Missing Image Alt Text Site-Wide
Evidence

Service icons, team images, and feature graphics across the site lack descriptive alt text. At least 9+ images on the homepage alone have no alt attributes.

Recommendation

Audit every image and add descriptive, keyword-relevant alt text. For service icons, describe the service. For decorative images, use empty alt="" attributes. This also improves accessibility compliance.

High Missing Open Graph & Social Meta Tags
Evidence

No Open Graph or Twitter Card meta tags detected. When pages are shared on social media, they appear without branded preview images or descriptions -- especially problematic for a social media marketing agency.

Recommendation

Configure Yoast SEO's social settings. Create branded OG images (1200x630px) for key pages. This is particularly important since you offer social media marketing as a service.

Medium Outdated Copyright and Design Elements
Evidence

Copyright notice reads "2021". The Google Ads page still references "Google Adwords" (rebranded to Google Ads in 2018). These small signals tell both Google and visitors the site isn't maintained.

Recommendation

Update copyright to 2026. Replace all "Adwords" references with "Google Ads". Use dynamic copyright year in the theme footer to prevent this from going stale again.
